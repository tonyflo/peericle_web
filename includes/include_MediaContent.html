Research Project: Samoan Passage Abyssal Mixing Expedition (SPAMEX)
February 15, 2014 7:43 PM

We're Underway!

We are measuring the turbulence in several of the channels of the Samoan Passage which is arguably the most important place for thermohaline mixing of North Atlantic Deep Water and Antarctic Bottom Water. This data will help climate forcasters in creating a more refined model of ocean circulation and dissolved carbon dioxide in the ocean. We hope to be completed with the third mooring cruise by the end of February 2014!
